How do I restore my purchase?
If you reinstalled the app or switched devices, tap the "Restore Purchases" button on the Pro upgrade screen. This will re-link your Apple ID to your ChromaFit account.

How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed by Apple. To cancel: Open Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > ChromaFit.

How do I request a refund?
As a developer, we do not have access to your billing information. All refunds are handled by Apple. Please visit reportaproblem.apple.com.

My steps/calories aren't syncing.

How accurate is the meal scanner?
ChromaFit uses advanced AI to estimate volume and calories. For the best results, ensure the photo is well-lit and the food is clearly visible. Turn on the "Greasy" toggle if the food was prepared with significant oil or butter.
