Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 23, 2026

Our Commitment: Your health and physique data are private. We do not sell your personal information to third parties or use it for targeted advertising.

1. Data We Collect

  • Health Data: With your permission, we read steps and active energy from Apple HealthKit. This data remains on your device or our secure servers solely for your dashboard display.
  • Photos: Physique and food photos are processed using secure AI models (Google Gemini) to provide analysis.

2. Use of AI

We use third-party AI providers (Google Gemini via OpenRouter) to analyze your photos. These images are used only for the purpose of providing your workout and nutrition insights. They are not used to train global AI models.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest. You may request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time via the app settings.
