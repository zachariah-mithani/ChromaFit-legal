Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 21, 2026

ChromaFit ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use the ChromaFit mobile application.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account Information: We collect your email address and name to sync your progress across devices and manage your subscription.
  • Fitness & Health Data: We collect user-provided data including height, weight, calorie intake, steps, and workout plans to provide the core tracking functionality of the app.
  • User Content (Photos): When you use the "Physique Lab" or "Food Tracker," we collect the photos you upload to perform AI-based analysis.
  • Identifiers: We collect anonymous device identifiers and user IDs to manage subscriptions via RevenueCat.

2. Third-Party AI Disclosure

Important: ChromaFit utilizes third-party AI services to analyze your physique and food photos.

  • Provider: We use OpenRouter to access Google Gemini models.
  • Data Shared: Only the specific photos you choose to analyze and your fitness goals are transmitted to these providers.
  • Purpose: This data is used solely to generate your physique critique or nutritional estimation.
  • No Training: Your personal data is not used by our third-party partners to train their AI models.

3. Subscriptions & Payments

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. When you purchase a "Pro" plan, RevenueCat receives a record of the transaction. We do not have access to your credit card or bank account details; all payments are processed securely by Apple.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data only for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time through the **Settings** menu in the app. Upon deletion, your photos and logs are permanently removed from our active databases.

5. Medical Disclaimer

ChromaFit is a wellness and fitness tool. The AI analysis provided is for aesthetic and informational purposes only. We do not provide medical diagnoses or treatment advice. Always consult a healthcare professional before starting a new diet or exercise regimen.
